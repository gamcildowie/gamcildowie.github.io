Verb List      Richard Serra's Verb List     Instagram Page

An exercise in process, inspired by the sculptor Richard Serra's 1967 "Verb List: Actions to Relate to Oneself". my aim was to use the editing software Photoshop and it's various tools to act out a list of verbs and actions. Then posting the findings on a Digital Publishing medium, of which I chose Instagram. Below is the list of actions I catalogued.
